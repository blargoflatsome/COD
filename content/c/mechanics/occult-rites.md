## What are they?
Occult Rites are homebrew spells performed by careful ritual. To enact a rite it may require multiple people, aquiring specific ingredients, performing various skill checks, being at a specific location, waiting for certain weather etc..

## Inspiration Cost
All Occult Rites require expending 1 or more Inspiration to enact. The Inspiration cost can be paid by multiple people so long as they are all involved in enacting the rite.

## How to Find Them
You will find Occult Rites in the game if you explore the occult or serve an occult entity (like a warlock patron). Anyone can learn and perform Occult Rites even if they normally can't cast spells.

## ... but what ARE they?
There is not a specific list of Occult Rites, they will be invented as the story requires them.
