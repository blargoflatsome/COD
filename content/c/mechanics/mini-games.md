## Cheating
Gain Advantage on any roll if you can find a way to cheat.

## Arm Wrestle
- Each contestant rolls a d20 and adds their Strength modifier.
- Highest total wins.

---

## Darts:
Roll 3 Dex Check. The closest grouping wins. You get 1 reroll per 2 Dex Bonus.

## Bronze Horde (dice game)
Premise: raid up to 18 coastal villages; your outing score is how many you raid.

**Rules:**
- Roll 3d6 as many times as you like to approach 18 without exceeding it.
- The roll closest to 18 (without going over) is your score for that outing.
- Score target: first player to reach exactly 100 points wins.
- Special: rolling three sixes in a single throw grants an additional +10 points (representing captured slaves).
